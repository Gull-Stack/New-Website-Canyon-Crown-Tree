Welcome to Canyon Crown Tree

Salt Lake City's Trusted Tree Care Experts

Founded in 2010 by Alex Rivera, an ISA-Certified Arborist with a forestry degree from Utah State University, Canyon Crown Tree has grown into one of the Wasatch Front's most respected tree care companies. We combine deep knowledge of Utah's native and ornamental tree species with industry-leading equipment and safety protocols.

Our team of certified professionals is committed to preserving the health and beauty of your trees while ensuring the safety of your property. Every job reflects our core values: expertise, integrity, and environmental stewardship.

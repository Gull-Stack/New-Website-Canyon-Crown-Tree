Stump Grinding

Home/Services/Stump Grinding

Reclaim Your Yard Space

After a tree is removed, the remaining stump can be an eyesore, a tripping hazard, and an invitation for pests. Our professional stump grinding service eliminates stumps quickly and efficiently, grinding them 6-12 inches below grade so you can replant, resod, or landscape the area.

We use commercial-grade grinders that handle stumps of any size, and we always clean up the wood chips — or leave them for you to use as mulch if you prefer.

What's Included

  • Stumps ground 6-12 inches below grade
  • Any size stump handled with commercial equipment
  • Root grinding available for surface roots
  • Chips cleaned up or left as mulch
  • Area ready for replanting or landscaping
  • Multi-stump discounts available

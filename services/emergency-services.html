When You Need Help — NOW

Utah storms don't wait for business hours, and neither do we. Our emergency tree service team is available 24/7, 365 days a year to respond to storm damage, fallen trees, and dangerous situations. We prioritize calls involving trees on structures, blocking roads, or threatening power lines.

Our crews are trained in emergency response protocols and carry the specialized equipment needed to handle the most challenging situations safely and quickly.

Emergency Services Include

  • 24/7/365 availability — no answering machines
  • Rapid response times — crews mobilized fast
  • Storm damage cleanup and debris removal
  • Trees on structures, cars, or fences
  • Power line clearance coordination with utilities
  • Insurance claim documentation and photos

⚠️ If a tree has contacted power lines, call Rocky Mountain Power (888-221-7070) first, then call us.

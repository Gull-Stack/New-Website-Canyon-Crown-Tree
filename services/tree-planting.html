The Right Tree, The Right Place

Planting the right tree in the right place is one of the best investments you can make in your property. Our arborists help you select species that thrive in Utah's unique climate and soil conditions, considering factors like mature size, water needs, sun exposure, and your aesthetic preferences.

We handle everything from site preparation to proper planting technique, and we provide aftercare guidance to give your new trees the best start. Popular choices for the Salt Lake City area include Gambel oak, quaking aspen, blue spruce, and honeylocust.

Planting Services

  • Species selection guidance from a certified arborist
  • Site analysis and preparation
  • Proper planting technique for long-term health
  • Aftercare instructions and watering schedule
  • Watering program setup
  • Growth monitoring available

Popular Trees for Utah

Shade Trees: Honeylocust, Hackberry, Kentucky Coffeetree, Red Oak — all drought-tolerant once established and well-suited to Utah's alkaline soils.

Ornamental Trees: Quaking Aspen, Japanese Maple, Serviceberry, Crabapple — beautiful seasonal interest with manageable size.

Evergreens: Blue Spruce, Austrian Pine, Rocky Mountain Juniper — year-round privacy and windbreaks native to the region.

Safe, Professional Tree Removal

Our certified arborists safely remove trees of all sizes using industry-leading techniques and equipment. Whether you have a storm-damaged tree threatening your home, a dead tree that's become a liability, or simply need to clear space for a new project, our crew handles it with precision and care.

We use rigging systems for confined spaces, cranes for large removals, and always prioritize protecting your property and landscaping. Every removal includes full debris cleanup and wood recycling.

What's Included

  • Hazardous tree assessment by a certified arborist
  • Crane-assisted removals for large or difficult trees
  • Confined space rigging techniques
  • Complete debris cleanup
  • Wood recycling and chip delivery available
  • Permit assistance when needed

Our Process

Every tree removal begins with a free on-site assessment. Our arborist evaluates the tree's condition, location, and any potential hazards, then provides a detailed written estimate. On removal day, our crew follows strict safety protocols — securing the work zone, using proper rigging, and protecting your property throughout the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

Cost depends on the tree's size, location, condition, and accessibility. Small trees may start around $300-500, while large or hazardous removals can range from $1,000 to $3,000+. We provide free written estimates so you know exactly what to expect.
Salt Lake City requires permits for certain tree removals, especially for trees in the public right-of-way. Our team handles permit research and can assist with the application process.
We recycle 100% of wood and green waste. Logs can be cut into firewood, chips are recycled as mulch, and we leave your property clean. We can also leave chips or firewood for you if desired.

Keep Your Trees Thriving

Healthy trees add beauty, shade, and value to your property. Our plant health care program combines proactive monitoring with targeted treatments to keep your trees in peak condition.

Our certified arborists diagnose issues ranging from nutrient deficiencies and drought stress to insect infestations and fungal diseases common in Utah, such as fireblight, iron chlorosis, and bark beetle damage. We use integrated pest management (IPM) strategies that minimize chemical use while maximizing tree health.

Health Care Services

  • Certified arborist diagnosis and assessment
  • Deep root fertilization
  • Insect and disease treatment
  • Iron chlorosis treatment (common in Utah soils)
  • Drought stress management
  • Ongoing monitoring programs

Common Utah Tree Problems

Iron Chlorosis: Utah's alkaline soils make iron unavailable to many trees, causing yellowing leaves. We treat this with soil injections and trunk applications that restore healthy green color.

Fireblight: A bacterial disease affecting fruit and ornamental trees. Early detection and proper pruning are critical for management.

Bark Beetles: Stressed trees are vulnerable to bark beetle infestations. Our IPM approach combines prevention and targeted treatment to protect your trees.

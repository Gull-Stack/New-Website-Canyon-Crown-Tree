## üìä COMPETITIVE INSIGHTS SUMMARY

### What ALL Top Sites Have:
1. **Years in business** prominently displayed (40-57 years)
2. **ISA Certification** and credentials
3. **Google reviews** or testimonials
4. **Free estimate CTA** (button visible above fold)
5. **Service area pages** or city lists (local SEO)
6. **Emergency services** mentioned
7. **FAQ section** addressing common questions

### Best SEO Practices Observed:
- City-specific landing pages (Diamond Tree)
- Service + Location in titles ("Tree Removal Salt Lake City")
- Blog/tips content for organic traffic
- Google review widgets embedded
- Schema markup for local business

### Differentiators That Work:
- Mulch/green waste side businesses (additional revenue)
- Permit handling (removes customer friction)
- Plant health care (upsell opportunity)
- HOA/commercial targeting (higher ticket)
- Equipment details (capability proof)
- Family/generational story (trust)
- Environmental angle (values alignment)

### Missing Opportunities (Most Sites):
- ‚ùå Before/after galleries (underused)
- ‚ùå Video content (almost none)
- ‚ùå Live chat/instant quote calculators
- ‚ùå Online booking/scheduling
- ‚ùå Financing options displayed
- ‚ùå Insurance claim assistance

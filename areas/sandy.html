Common Tree Challenges in Sandy

Snow & Ice Damage

Sandy's proximity to the mountains means heavy snow loads. We prune trees to reduce weight and prevent branch failure during winter storms.

Canyon Winds

Winds funneling down Little Cottonwood Canyon can exceed 80 mph. Our structural pruning helps trees withstand these powerful gusts.

Iron Chlorosis

Like much of the Salt Lake Valley, Sandy's alkaline soils cause yellowing leaves in maples and oaks. We provide effective treatments.

Trees Near Structures

Growing communities mean trees planted too close to homes. We safely remove hazardous trees and recommend proper replacements.

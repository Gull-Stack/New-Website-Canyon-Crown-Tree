Draper's Unique Tree Care Challenges

Steep Terrain

SunCrest and Hidden Canyon lots require specialized equipment and techniques. Our crew is experienced in hillside removals with proper rigging and safety protocols.

Fire Mitigation

Draper's mountain interface areas face wildfire risk. We provide defensible space clearing and fire-resistant landscaping recommendations.

Wildlife Damage

Deer, elk, and other wildlife can damage trees. We protect young trees and repair damage from rubbing and browsing.

New Development

Proper tree planting in new construction is critical. We help homeowners select and plant trees suited to Draper's conditions.

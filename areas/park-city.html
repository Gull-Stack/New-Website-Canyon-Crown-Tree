Mountain Tree Care Challenges

Heavy Snow Loads

Park City's famous powder can break branches and topple trees. We provide structural pruning and snow removal to protect your property.

Wildfire Mitigation

Creating defensible space is critical in Park City. We thin forests, remove ladder fuels, and help you meet fire code requirements.

Aspen Care

Park City's iconic aspens require specialized knowledge. We manage aspen groves, treat fungal diseases, and protect these beautiful trees.

Pine Beetle

Mountain pine beetles threaten Park City's conifers. Early detection and removal of infected trees protects your forest.

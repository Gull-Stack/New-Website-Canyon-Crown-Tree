Common Tree Problems in Salt Lake City

Utah's unique climate presents specific challenges for tree health.

Iron Chlorosis

Salt Lake's alkaline soils cause iron deficiency in maples, oaks, and other trees. We provide soil treatments and trunk injections to restore tree health.

Wind Damage

Utah's canyon winds can snap branches and topple trees. We provide 24/7 storm response and preventive pruning to reduce wind resistance.

Drought Stress

Our semi-arid climate stresses trees, especially during hot summers. We offer deep root watering and proper mulching to help trees thrive.

Pest Infestations

Ips beetles, borers, and aphids threaten Utah trees. Our arborists diagnose infestations early and implement integrated pest management.
